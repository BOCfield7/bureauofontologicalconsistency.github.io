About the Bureau

The Bureau of Ontological Consistency (B.O.C.) serves as a stabilizing archival node across variant threads. Our mission is to observe, log, and, when necessary, intervene in recursive events, recursion bleed, temporal dissonance, and other non-causal phenomena.
